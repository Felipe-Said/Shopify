click "New Product" below "Dashboard"
generate unique name , then enter into "Name" and save as "productName"
generate from template "********" , then enter into "SKU" and save as "productSku"
generate from template "1#.##", then enter into "Price" and save as "productPrice"
enter "1" into "Weight"
enter "50" into "Quantity"
select "Black" from dropdown on right of "Color"
select "SM" from dropdown on right of "Size"
enter stored value "productName" into "Url key"
enter stored value "productName" into "Meta title"
click "Save" until page contains "Product updated successfully"
click "close"
